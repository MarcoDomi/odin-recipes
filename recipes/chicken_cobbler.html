Chicken Cobbler

Description

A hearty chicken cobbler

A warm and comfy chicken cobbler that is the perfect way to unwind after a hard day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cups shredded chicken
  • 15 ounce frozen veggies
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can chicken soup cream
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup whipping cream
  • 1 package cheddar biscuit mix

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Place shredded chicken and veggies into dish. season with thyme, pepper, salt and pour soup, broth and cream and stir.
  3. Prepare biscuit mix
  4. Drop 8 biscuit dough portions into chicken and veggie mixture
  5. bake in oven for 40 minutes and season with thyme.
