Lasagna

Description

A recipe for a delicious homemade lasagna that's both quick and easy!

Lasagna can be tough to make but this recipe is both beginner friendly and a crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced Onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until browned
  3. stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste and water. add sugar, parsley, basil, salt, italian seasonging, fennel seeds and pepper. Simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Bring pot of salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. Combine ricotta cheese with egg in mixing bowl
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  7. Spread meat sauce over bottom of baking dish. Place lasagna noodles on top of sauce and place cheese mixture on-top of noodles. Place more meat sauce on-top of cheese mixture.
  8. Repeat layers and cheese. Cover with foil
  9. Bake in oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
