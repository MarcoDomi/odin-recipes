Meatloaf

Description

A traditional homemade meatloaf.

Sometimes it's good to go back to basics and enjoy the simpler things in life. Meatloaf is the perfect recipe for unwinding after a stressful day. This recipe is beginner friendly and requires a small number of ingredients making it ideal for people following a budget.

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 1/2 pounds ground chuck
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon italian herbs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup bread crumbs

    • Glaze ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  3. Place carrot, celery, onion, bell pepper, mushrooms and garlic in blender until it is turned into a puree.
  4. Add ground chuck to vegetables, add worcestershire sauce, egg, italian herbs, salt, black pepper and cayenne. Mix gently along with bread crumbs.
  5. Shape mixture into a loaf. Place in baking pan.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until hot for about 15 minutes
  7. Mix brown sugar, ketchup, Dijon and sriracha
  8. Remove meatloaf from oven and glaze
  9. Return to oven and bake for 45-75 minutes.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
